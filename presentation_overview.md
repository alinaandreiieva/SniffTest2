# SniffTest Presentation Overview

## Onboarding View
This is the app's introduction and disclaimer flow. It explains that SniffTest is an educational tool for recognizing disinformation techniques, sets expectations around AI-generated content and probabilistic outputs, and prepares the user before entering the app.

## Quiz View
This is the main learning space of the app. Users move through three levels of difficulty, starting with simple true-or-bluff decisions, then identifying specific disinformation techniques, and finally judging whether images are real or AI-generated under time pressure.

## Room View
This view turns media literacy into a social game. Users can create or join a room, simulate a small group session, and play a "Truth / Bluff" round where statements are discussed and checked with an animated AI-style validation reveal.

## Legal View
This view presents the legal and ethical framework behind the app. It explains age guidance, educational purpose, AI transparency, privacy principles, and the European and Ukrainian legal context that shapes the project.

## Profile View
This is the personal progress space of the app. Users can customize their nickname and avatar, track stats and badges, switch language settings, and keep a lightweight local profile without needing full registration.
